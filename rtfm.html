Notes

  • The weapon skill mechanics are implemented assuming the information in this source is correct.
  • All weapon damage is averaged, there is no point in wasting time RNGing it.
  • The code makes a number of assumptions that are not necessarily verified yet:
    • Rage is a floating point number, rage gains and losses are not rounded to whole numbers
    • Extra swings like Hand of Justice and Windfury totem are regular attacks, that suffer the dual-wield hit penalty and can be glancing blows
    • Extra swings like Hand of Justice and Windfury totem do *not* consume Flurry charges
    • The attack power buff from Windfury Totem does not apply to other swings that happen a short time after
    • All procs, including extra attacks always happen before the actual event that triggered them. In particular, an attack will profit form the attack power buff of a Crusader proc that it itself caused
    • Extra attack procs cannot proc each other, although they can all proc on the same event
    • Dodged and parried white swings generate 75% of the rage that a normal hit would have
    • Glancing blows generate rage proportional to their damage, which means depending on your weapon skill, a glancing blow could generate less rage than a dodge
    • Slam rage costs are checked at the start of the cast, but the rage is subtracted at the end of the cast
    • All abilities except Heroic strike and Whirlwind refund 84% of their rage costs if they are dodged/parried but not when they miss.
  • Things that are working so far:
    • Basic setup with UI, Web Worker and main loop
    • Combat mechanics, including target level, weapon skill etc.
    • Raw damage calculations for all common Fury specs
    • Basic rage mechanics (need to double check the numbers)
    • Most commonly used damage Abilites
    • Two-hand weapons
    • Hand of Justice, Windfury Totem and Crusader enchant
    • Parsing talent spec from a link
    • Anger Management and Unbridled Wrath
    • Basic logging and results
  • Lots of things are still missing. In particular:
    • Some relevant procs (Thrash Blade, Flurry Axe, Lifestealing, Demonslaying...)
    • Overpower, Deep Wounds, Bloodrage and Deathwish
    • Arms and Prot specs
    • Attacking from the front
    • Armor and armor reduction (Bonereaver's Edge, Annihilator etc.)
    • Heroic Strike rank IX from Dire Maul
    • Daggers
    • Execute does not yet refund rage on misses
    • Blessing of Kings (relevant for Crusader proc)
    • ...
